This webinar is designed for you — choose your lens

For Grantmakers

Make better grantmaking decisions with
coordinated community insight.

Foundations already collect feedback — but it's often fragmented, one-off, and difficult to act on. This session introduces an ecosystem-level approach so you and your partners can generate meaningful insight without adding burden.

You'll learn how Matter of Voice can help

  • Coordinate qualitative insight across grantees and partners
  • Capture lived experience at scale without increasing reporting burden
  • Turn stories into actionable themes for decision-making
  • Apply this approach across convenings, evaluations, and strategy

For Nonprofits

Turn community voice into
influence, funding, and stronger programs.

Nonprofits are closest to the communities they serve — but the full depth of that experience is often hard to capture, scale, and communicate in ways that drive funding, influence policy, and improve programs.

You'll learn how Matter of Voice can help

  • Amplify community voice at scale to support advocacy and policy change
  • Build more compelling funding narratives grounded in lived experience
  • Create shared infrastructure for story collection across partners
  • Gather actionable feedback to improve program design and adoption

For Associations

Turn network voice into shared insight and
influence across your entire ecosystem.

Associations sit at the center of powerful networks — but much of the insight across chapters, members, and partners remains siloed, underutilized, or difficult to act on.

You'll learn how Matter of Voice can help

  • Enhance traditional surveys with qualitative insight that surfaces blind spots
  • Equip chapters with shared intelligence to assess local needs and apply what's working elsewhere
  • Amplify network voice at scale to support advocacy and policy influence
  • Coordinate insight across your network — not just at headquarters
