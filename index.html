The Challenge

Organizations are rich in stories.
Strategy still runs
on assumption.

Grantmakers and nonprofits have more access to community data than ever before. But collection remains time and resource intensive, just to accomplish the minimum. The problem is that today's stories are difficult to pass along. By the time it reaches the people making decisions, stories are often reduced to pull quotes or summaries.

Today, community voice arrives fragmented, unanalyzed, and too late. Boards plan without it. Proposals are written around it. The gap between what communities experience and what organizations act on widens each cycle. True impact comes when these voices arrive intact and joined by many others.

Signal doesn't survive the handoff

Stories collected by one team rarely reach another. Program staff, development, and strategy operate on different versions of community reality.

The most important voices are hardest to reach

Traditional engagement methods favor those with time and proximity to formal settings. Communities most affected by your work are the least represented in your data.

Analysis is always a sprint before a deadline

By the time qualitative data is coded and presented, the planning cycle has moved on. Real-time community signal is not a luxury. It is the condition for relevant strategy.

Collection without reciprocity erodes trust

Communities share experiences and receive nothing in return. Over time, participation declines precisely when organizational learning depends on it.

How It Works

Four collection benefits.
One real-time signal.

From first story to strategic insight, the platform is built so that community voice moves through your organization instead of pooling in a single team member's inbox or stagnates paraphrased in a shared file.

01

Collect, without adding burden

Share a link or QR code. Community members respond on their phone, in their own language, in their own time. No account. No download. No staff hand-holding needed.

02

Analyze in real time

Stories are anonymized, grouped into themes, and made filterable by issue, geography, and demographics the moment they arrive. No manual coding. No waiting for a report.

03

Share across your ecosystem

You choose who can join and contribute to building a shared story pool. Every team draws from the same source of truth. Reporting, proposals, and strategy work from the same evidence base.

04

Deepen through ongoing dialogue

Invite contributors into compensated advisory panels for follow-up questions and longitudinal learning. Your community dialog builds over time rather than starting over each cycle.
