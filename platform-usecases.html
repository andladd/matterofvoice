Collection

Stories from the communities
you actually need to hear from.

Share a link or QR code. Community members respond in their own words, in most languages, on their own phone, in their own time. No account. No download. No staff hand-holding needed. Stories arrive immediately.

  • Mobile-first, link and QR code delivery
  • Anonymous by default — no personal information collected through standard participation
  • Available in most languages with automatic translation
  • Participants see emerging themes from others before they submit, making the exchange reciprocal
  • No account creation, email address, or app download required
  • Voluntary and consent-based — plain-language consent before each submission
Reach participants that surveys and phone outreach miss
Analysis & Reporting

Board-ready insight,
without the analysis sprint.

Stories are anonymized, grouped into themes, and made filterable by issue, geography, and demographics the moment they arrive. No manual coding. No waiting until the end of a grant cycle to see what people are saying.

  • Real-time theme identification across all collected stories
  • Filterable by issue area, location, demographic, and time period
  • Spam filtering and automatic removal of identifying information
  • One-click report generation for funders, boards, and strategy sessions
  • Compare themes across different population groups or geographies
  • Export formats appropriate for grant reporting and board presentations
Analysis that runs continuously, not once before a deadline
Partner Sharing

One collection effort.
Organization-wide utility.

Partners and grantees contribute to a shared story pool. Every team draws from the same source of truth. Program staff, development, communications, and strategy work from the same evidence base rather than building separate data silos.

  • Multi-organization story pooling with role-based access controls
  • Team and partner management tools that distribute collection prompts across your network
  • Auto-generated outreach content for text, email, and social media distribution
  • Grantees contribute without managing their own platform instances
  • Shared insight surfaces across strategy, programs, communications, and development
  • Reduce duplicated community outreach across your partner network
Community voice becomes an organizational resource, not a team asset
Community Panels

Deeper dialogue.
Ongoing learning.

Community Panels is an optional feature that lets organizations invite story contributors into compensated advisory panels. Deeper engagement, follow-up questions, and longitudinal insight — without repeated extraction.

Panels are built for Research and Evaluation teams that need two-way dialogue, not just submission data.

Targeted recruitment from existing contributors

Invite participants whose stories match your research focus. Automated outreach handled by the platform.

Asynchronous dialogue via mobile

Follow-up questions delivered to participants on their phones. No scheduling, no facilitated sessions required.

Compensated participation

Pay participants for their time through the platform. Compensation management handled without additional administrative burden.

Cumulative learning over time

Panel relationships persist. Organizations build longitudinal understanding rather than starting over each cycle.
